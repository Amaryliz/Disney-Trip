When you're planning the perfect Disney vacation there's a lot of thought has to go into EVERY detail, especially when it comes to picking which hotel to stay in and when it's best to purchase tickets.

Hotels:

In Orlando Disney has over 20 hotels that you can choose from, which can make choosing difficult! But we can narrow down our scope and choose the perfect option for us by looking at the different categories of hotels that Disney offers; Value, Moderate, and Deluxe Resorts

Value Resorts

Value Resorts are the PERFECT option if you are having a family vacation with childern and you're on a budget. The Value Resorts typically range $100-$200 a night, which is prefect if you're on a budget, and the hotels are decorated with characters! They also have food court dining, which is kinda like cafeteria style so it's the perfect grab and go for a busy family. There are some downsides to staying at Value resorts, one of them being that in order to get to the parks you have to wait 10-20 minutes for the bus to pick you up to take you to the parks if you dont have a car. Also, there's NO waterslides!!

Moderate Resorts:

Going higher on the list we have Moderate Resorts. So these resorts are a bit more pricey and range between $200-$300 a night, and they have a bit of luxury to them. The rooms are a little bigger than the value rooms, and they have king size bed options. These resorts also have BOTH food court and table service!! So if one day you're in the mood to sit down and enjoy a glass of wine you can. To top it off theres WATERSLIDES!!! But just like Value Resorts the only way to get to the parks is by bus. Unless you stay at the Port Orleans Riverside or French Quarter you can get a water taxi!!

Deluxe Resorts:

Lastly we have the Deluxe Resorts! These bad boys have a price tag of $350-$700 a night, but the luxury maybe worth it for some. The rooms are big and make you feel like you have been whisked off to somewhere far away. Your food options here are food court, table service, OR Character Dining, breakfast with Mickey!! Also, there's extra amenities such as on-site gyms, salons, valet parking, and some resorts have kid activity centers! As for transportaion you have multiple options on how to get to the parks- boats, bus, monorail, or even walking.
