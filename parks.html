I dont think that choosing which park you want to go to is difficult, but knowing how to navigate is a different story. So, I'm going to give some tips and tricks to make walking around the parks bareable.

The biggest tip I can give to anyone is DO NOT SPEND MONEY ON WATER BOTTLES!! The reasons that I say this is because One they're OVER priced and two you can get a free water cup from any resturant at the parks. So save that money!


My second tip is don't waste money on the park hopper pass. I just think it's a waste of money, because it's hard to enjoy an entire park in just one day so imagine trying to enjoy two. Also, if you're planning a Disney trip for a couple of days you'll most likely be going to the parks every day.


Another extra that I would skip out on would be the line hopper pass, but only during certain seasons. During peak seasons like summer and winter break the line hopper is useful. But during slow season you really don't need it, because Disney actually allows you to reserve three rides to skip the line at certain times. So why pay for something that you can get for free?


The last tip is BE COMFORTABLE! Wear comfy shoes and clothes, because you are going to be walking around a big ole Disney park for over 8 hours. I promise you will not last in flats!
